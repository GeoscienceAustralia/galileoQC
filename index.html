Airborne Gravity Quality Control¶

galileoQC is a Python package for quality assurance and control of airborne gravity[1] data. It covers both gravimetry and gradiometry from a range of instrument types and was designed for use in QC of data collected under contracts based on the Geoscience Australia Airborne Gravity Deed [2]. It has also proved useful in the QC of many other airborne gravity surveys. Some functions are provided for the QC of aeromagnetic data.

Notes and references¶

[1]

Throughout this documentation, “gravity” is used in a general sense to refer to both the acceleration (measured by a gravimeter) and the gradient (measured by a gravity gradiometer) of the gravity field.

[2]

See, similarly: J. A. Goodwin. Airborne magnetic and radiometric technical standards. Geoscience Australia, Record 2023(4), 2023. doi: 10.26186/147457.
