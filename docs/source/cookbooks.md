## Data Acknowledgements

The tutorial uses field data from airborne gravity surveys to demonstrate the use of AirGravQC on real data. Permission to use field data has been kindly given by:

Sander Geophysics: airborne gravimetry data extracts ("AirGrav") from two large surveys flown in 2022-2023 in Victoria and New South Wales, Australia;

Xcalibur Multiphysics: airborne gravity gradiometry data ("Falcon") from the Canobie survey (2021) flown in Queensland, Australia; and
	
BellGeo: airborne gravity gradiometry data ("AirFTG") from the Vinton Dome survey (2008) flown in Louisiana, USA.
