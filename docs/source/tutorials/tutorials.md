## Eastern Victoria, Australia

The Eastern Victoria airborne gravimeter survey was flown by Sander Geophysics. Details can be found at [^EastVicRefs]. This notebook demonstrates many of the QC tools used for airborne gravimetry.

```{toctree}
QC_EastVic.ipynb
```

## Canobie, Queensland, Australia

The Canobie airborne gravity gradiometer survey was flown by Xcalibur. Details can be found at [^CanobieRefs]. This notebook demonstrates many of the QC tools used for surveys flown with a single-axis, dual-complement, rotating gradiometer.

```{toctree}
QC_Canobie.ipynb
```

## Vinton Dome, Louisiana, USA

The Vinton Dome airborne gravity gradiometer survey was flown by Bell Geospace. Details can be found at [^Murphy2004]. This notebook demonstrates many of the QC tools used for surveys flown with a 3-axis, single-complement, rotating gradiometer.

```{toctree}
QC_VintonDome.ipynb
```

[^Murphy2004]: C. A. Murphy and G. R. Mumaw. 3D Full Tensor Gradiometry: a high resolution gravity measuring instrument resolving ambiguous geological interpretations. In ASEG-PESA 17th Geophysical Conference and Exhibition, pages 1–4, Sydney, July 2004.
