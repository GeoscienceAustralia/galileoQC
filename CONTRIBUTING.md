# Contributing to __galileoQC__

We welcome contributions to __galileoQC__, currently only in the form of suggestions for enhancements. Additional types of contributions should be possible in the future. This document sets out our guidelines and best practices for such contributions.

It's based on the [Contributing to Open Source Projects Guide](https://contribution-guide-org.readthedocs.io/).

## Suggesting Enhancements

We welcome suggestions for enhancements, but reserve the right to reject them if they do not follow future plans for __galileoQC__.
